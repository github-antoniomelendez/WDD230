Discover Torreon

Population

  • The total population of Torreón in 2020 was 720,848 inhabitants, with 51.2% woman, and 48.8% men.
  • The age ranges that concentrated the largest population were 15 to 19 years (60,741 inhabitants), 20 to 24 years (60,416 inhabitants), and 10 to 14 years (59,758 inhabitants). Among them they concentrated 25.1% of the total population.
